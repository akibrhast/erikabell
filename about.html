Erika Bell is a Los Angeles-based composer drawn to intuition, preference and the personal. The combination of these things filtered through their compositional process often results in works that are spacious, textural and melancholy. Their work is lightly focused on textural construction, irregular ensemble groupings and a structure-based approach. Bell has had works performed in Sweden, U.K, New York and—in Los Angeles—the Walt Disney Hall, Zebulon, The Dog Star Festival, the wulf. Human Resources and more. Bell also performs in Erika Bell, Picky Bunches, The Ensemble Whose Name is Uhhhhhmm, Cali Bellow and other occasional projects. Erika holds a BFA in music composition from California Institute of the Arts where they studied with Michael Pisaro and Laura Steenberge.
